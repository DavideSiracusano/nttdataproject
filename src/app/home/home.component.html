URBAN CITY LIFE

NTT Data Project The Urban Life Project, in collaboration with s2impact University

The company that commissioned the development of this application aims to improve the lives of people in urban centers, so that cities become increasingly centers for new ideas, commerce, culture, science, productivity, and social development. The application will be free, allowing people to register and share their ideas and reports via posts, primarily contributing to:

  • •Protecting and safeguarding the city's cultural heritage.
  • •Protecting and safeguarding the city's natural heritage.
  • •Supporting positive social and environmental connections between urban areas.

"The application is part of a start2impact university project in collaboration with NTT Data. To access it, registration or login is required, followed by careful adherence to the required steps."
